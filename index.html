EcoStreak

Created EcoStreak at Camp Polyhacks, a Cal Poly hackathon. Over 24 hours, my team of 6 ideated, designed, and built a prototype for an iOS application. The application, EcoStreak, aims to engage Cal Poly students in being eco friendly. Students are given a daily challenge, and submit a Snapchat-esque clip of them completing the challenge. They can view submissions of their peers. They are rewarded based on their streaks, or sequential days of completing challenges.

Our team received the grand prize, as well as an award from Pariveda Solutions.

contribu

Participated in the Abortion Access Hackathon over one weekend. Worked with another developer to create contribu, an iOS app that gamifies donating to abortion funds. We were featured on CNN. A user is presented a weekly challenge posted by NNAF. If they choose to opt into the challenge, they pledge an amount they will donate to NNAF every time they do the activity in the challenge. For example, if the challenge is to "donate every time you watch Netflix," a user will pledge to donate a specified amount to NNAF every time they watch Netflix that week. With Facebook login and integration, users can compete with their friends and view the challenges they completed and number of times they donated.
